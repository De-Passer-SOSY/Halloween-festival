Halloween festival Brugge

28/10 t.e.m. 31/10

This autumn, the Historium is celebrating Halloween again. From Friday 9 October till Wednesday 28 October*, a hundred pumpkins, cobwebs and bats will make for a spooky atmosphere. Real daredevils can extend their visit with the Halloween Virtual Reality Experience or a visit to the Torture Museum. If you come dressed in your most beautiful Halloween costume, you will enjoy a € 1 discount! For the kids there is also a nice Halloween Trail: a family trail with Halloween Quiz and a nice surprise… trick or treat!
